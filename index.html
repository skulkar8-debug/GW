Use Case Scenarios

Quarterly Board Review

CFO needs to present Q3 actuals vs budget to the board. Using the Executive Dashboard, they pull an Income Statement showing variance analysis by department, highlighting that Sales came in 8% over budget while Product stayed on track.

Annual Budget Planning

Finance team collaborates with department heads to build next year's budget. Each department manager logs into their Budget User Dashboard to input their operating expense forecasts, eliminating back-and-forth spreadsheet emails.

Headcount Expansion Decision

VP of Sales wants to hire 3 new account executives in Q2. Using Workforce FTE Planning, they model the fully-loaded costs (salary, benefits, equipment) and see the impact on the operating expense forecast before getting approval.

Rolling 12-Month Forecast Update

At the start of each quarter, the finance team updates the rolling forecast with latest actuals from Sage Intacct. The Revenue Planning module automatically adjusts future months based on current trends, giving leadership a continuously updated view.
